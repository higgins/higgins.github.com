Yearly Goals

"If I had a dollar for everytime I said that...I'd be making money in a very weird way." -- Mitch Hedberg

Every year I give $1 a prize to anyone who challenges themselves to achieve something by the end of the year.

Here's how it works:

  1. Think of a goal
  2. Fill out this form
  3. Chat with me to loosely confirm your goal is SMART
  4. Do it... make your dreams come true
  5. If you don't, you don't get the prize

That's it. Easy peasy. Do something fun, learn, grow and win a small prize.

The deadline to confirm your goals is January 21st, 2026.

Don't know what a good goal is? Check out the participants from previous years.
