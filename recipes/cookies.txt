Name: Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:
 - peanut butter (1c)
 - almond flour (2c)
 - maple syrup (1/3c)

Directions:
 1. Mix 2c almond flour, 1c peanut butter, 1/3c maple syrup
 2. Let sit for 30min
 3. Form into balls about 2tbsp in volume
 4. Heat oven to 350F
 5. Bake for 10min
 6. Cool for 10min
