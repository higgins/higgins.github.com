Name: Sauerkraut

Ingredients:
 - cabbage
 - salt
 - clean vessel
 - clean weight

Directions:
 1. Chop or shread cabbage into small pieces
 2. Weigh cabbage
 3. Measure our 2% weight of cabbage in salt
 4. Massage salt into shredded cabbage
 5. Store in vessel
 6. Mash cabbage below juices
 7. Cover with water with 2% salinity
 8. Weight cabbage down with weight and execess cabbage leaves
 9. Skim off mold from top if it appears
 10. Let sit for 11 days, burp it when necessary
