Name: Cold Bean Salad

Ingredients:
 - Black beans (16oz)
 - Cucumber
 - Bell peppers
 - Red onion (to taste)
 - Cherry tomatoes
 - Basil
 - Oil
 - Vinegar
 - Mustard
 - Avocado (topping)

Directions:
 1. Chop it all up
 2. Mix oil, vinegar and mustard
 3. Pour over chopped salad
 4. Mix
 5. Add avocado per serving (not mixed unless you're eating it all)
