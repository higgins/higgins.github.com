Name: Vegan Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Ingredients:
 - 3/4 cup raw cashews
 - 1 small onion, diced
 - 1 tblspn minced garlic
 - 1 lb potatoes (yellow or brown) peeled and cubed (3-4 medium-sized potatoes)
 - 2 carrots peeled and chopped
 - 6 cups vegetable broth
 - 1/2 cup nooch
 - 4 cups broccoli florrets
 - 1/2 lemon juice
 - 1 tsp smoked paprika

Directions:
 1. Cook onion then garlic
 2. Add potatoes and broth, cook until fork tender
 3. Sift out half potatoes, blend with soaked nuts. Add water if needed
 4. Add broccoli, carrots, nooch and paprika to pot, simmer for 5-10min
 5. Add blended potato
 6. Cook together a bit more
 7. Salt to taste
 8. Make some bread with it
