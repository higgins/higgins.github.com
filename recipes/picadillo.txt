Name: Picadillo

Ingriedients:
 - onion
 - potatoes
 - garlic cloves
 - ground turkey
 - tomato sauce
 - white rice
 - (optional) olives

Directions:
 1. Cook rice
 2. Dice onion and garlic
 3. Dice potatoes into cubic centimeters
 4. Cook onion until soft then add garlic
 5. Cook potatoes with onion and garlic
 6. Once potatoes are cooked, remove from heat
 7. Cook ground turkey until browned
 8. Mix tomato sauce into turkey
 9. Cook for a few minutes
 10. Mix cooked tomaotoes into turkey and tomato sauce
 11. Season to taste
