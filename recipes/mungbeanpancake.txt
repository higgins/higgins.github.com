Name: Mung Bean Pancake

Ingredients:
 - split yellow mung beans (aka moong dal) (1 cup)
 - nut milk or water (1 cup)
 - black salt / kala namak (1 tsp)
 - salt (0.5 tsp)
 - olive oil (2 tbsp)
 - tumeric (0.5 tsp)
 - garlic powder (0.5 tsp)
 - nutritional yeast (3 tbsp)
 - black pepper (to taste)
 - paprika (to taste)

Directions:
 1. Soak beans in water for 24+ hours
 2. Blend it all until smooth
 3. Cook in pan
